Database Information

Database: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/nezukokamaado/road-accident-casualties-dataset
Each row represents an instance of a car crash with the following information: TO DO FIX

  • Index: The title of each book
  • Accident_Severity: The title of each book
  • Accident Date: The name of the author(s)
  • Latitude: The code representing the language the book was written in
  • Light_Conditions: The rating assigned to the author (Excellent, Intermediate, Famous, Novice)
  • District Area: The average rating given to the book by readers
  • Longitude: The quanitity of ratings given to the book by readers
  • Number_of_Casualties: The genre or category of the book (children, fiction, nonfiction)
  • Number_of_Vehicles: The total sales revenue generated by each book
  • Road_Surface_Conditions: The revenue earned by publishers from selling each book
  • Road_Type: The price to purchase one copy of the book
  • Urban_or_Rural_Area: A numeric value indicating a book's rank based on its sales performance in comparison to other books within its category (genre)
  • Weather_Conditions: Total number of copies sold
  • Vehicle_Type: Total number of copies sold
